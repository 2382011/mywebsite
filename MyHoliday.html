Hello, my name is Elsa Yunita Sihotang. I want to tell you about an event during my holiday. It's about My Job. I come from Riau. I have 3 brothers. I want to tell you about my vacation when I was on holiday from college for 3 months. Well, at first I didn't intend and didn't think about returning to my hometown because of my family's finances, and I didn't have the heart. but my mother told me to go home and help with work at home and in the market.

Because I couldn't bear to see my mother selling alone without anyone helping, and I went back to my village. Actually, my father and sister can help but only on Sundays because on Thursdays my father works and my brother goes to school. And when one month ended I intended to work, I said to my mother "Mom, I want to work". Because my mother has a subscription to a food stall, namely pecal catfish. Finally I worked.

I started working in mid-June and time passed.at the beginning of August I finished work. So every day I help my mother with housework,and on Thursday and Sunday morning I help my mother at the market. And in the afternoon I go to work at the food stall, from 5 pm to 11 pm. and I returned to Bandung on August 14.
